# Chapter 14 Outline: Education and Community

## Purpose
Explore the educational resources, learning paths, and community-driven development culture that underpin the OpenClaw ecosystem and the broader AI-native movement.

## Overview
This chapter examines how individuals can learn AI-native development, contribute to the community, and help foster an inclusive, innovative environment. We'll analyze the AI-first contribution pattern, learning resources, and future of AI-native education.
